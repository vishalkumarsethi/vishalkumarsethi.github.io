Thoughts: 

Patriots vs. Chiefs goodness me. Tom Brady is pretty good at this football thing. 

Was talking with someone about the idea of "sacrifice now for better later" or "enjoy now at the expense of later". This idea can be worded in an infinite amount of ways of course. An example that may relate to students (rather cliche) is to spend a night studying or go party.  It is very hard to quantify the gain from 1, 2, or x hours of studying vs. a night with potentially high levels of fun and novelty, but most people would agree that hard things take time, and that time needs to be put in somewhere e.g. habits. 

Right now I'm not certain on which is better; pretty sure it would vary per the situation. 

Pro Tip: Keeping a bottle of Purell/your choice of hand sanitizer on a desk or work area or in a high traffic area is VERY worth. 
The benefits of being able to sanitize your hands easily and in a very conveinent manner outweighs the extra cost. A 10oz bottle will last you a whole 365 days EASILY, but likely 2+ years. 
Also, when your hands feel dry or "dirty", it seems to fix that problem too. 

If not, then a sink and some hand soap will be good too. 
Don't wanna get sick during flu/cold/sniffle season! 

Also, don't forget to stay warm in these colder months! 
Nothing beats a warm puffy jacket and some gloves.
